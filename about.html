Our Beginning

MelonKids was born from a simple idea: what if we could make children feel truly special by making them the hero of their own story? Founded in 2025, we've dedicated ourselves to creating personalized books that spark imagination, foster a love for reading, and create lasting memories.

Our team of writers, illustrators, and AI engineers work together to create books that are not only personalized with a child's name, but actually feature them as the main character through our innovative AI technology.

With just a few photos, we can transform your child into the hero of a magical adventure, a brave explorer, or whatever they dream of becoming. Our books are designed to inspire confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love of reading.

Our Mission

At MelonKids, our mission is to create joy through personalized books - magical adventures for kids and touching tributes for adults. We believe that stories have the power to connect us, inspire us, and help us celebrate the special moments in life.

We're committed to creating high-quality, beautifully illustrated books that make people feel truly seen and celebrated. Whether it's a child's first birthday or a couple's 50th anniversary, we're here to help you create meaningful gifts that will be treasured for years to come.

Personalization

We believe each person's story is unique and worth celebrating. Our books are tailored to reflect the individual's personality and appearance.

Quality

We're committed to creating beautiful, durable books with premium materials and stunning illustrations that can be passed down through generations.

Imagination

We foster creativity and wonder through our stories and illustrations, encouraging children to dream big and explore new worlds.

Privacy

We handle your personal photos with the utmost care and respect. Your privacy is our priority, and we maintain strict security protocols to protect your data.

Customer Satisfaction

We're only happy when you're thrilled with your book. Our dedicated support team ensures every customer has an exceptional experience from start to finish.
