Frequently Asked Questions

How does the personalization process work?

Our personalization process is simple: upload a few clear photos of your child or loved one, and our AI technology will transform them into beautiful illustrations that are incorporated throughout the story. You can also add their name and customize certain elements of the story to make it even more special.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the United States. International shipping can take 10-14 business days. We also offer expedited shipping options at checkout for those who need their books more quickly.

What is your refund policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your book for any reason, please contact us within 30 days of receiving it, and we'll either replace it or issue a full refund. Please note that personalized books cannot be returned, but we'll work with you to make it right.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. We take data privacy very seriously. The photos you upload are used solely for creating your personalized book and are stored securely. We do not share your personal data with third parties, and we have stringent security measures in place to protect your information. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

What age groups are your books suitable for?

We have books for children of all ages, from babies to early readers (ages 0-8). Each book is designed with a specific age range in mind, with appropriate content, language, and illustrations. Our adult books are perfect for partners, parents, or friends of any age, and make meaningful gifts for special occasions.
