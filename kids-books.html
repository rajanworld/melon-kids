Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personalized books

How long does it take to receive my personalized book?

After you upload your photos and complete your order, our team will create your personalized book within 2-3 business days. Shipping typically takes an additional 3-4 business days within the US, or 7-10 days for international orders.

What kind of photos work best for personalization?

Clear, well-lit photos where your child's face is visible work best. We recommend uploading 3-5 different photos from different angles. Our AI works with most photos, but higher quality images will result in better illustrations.

Can I preview my book before it's printed?

Yes! After our AI creates the illustrations, you'll receive a digital proof of your book to review before printing. You can request adjustments if needed.

What is the quality of the books?

Our books are printed on high-quality, durable paper with vibrant colors. They feature hardcover binding designed to withstand years of reading. Each book is approximately 8.5" x 8.5" with 24-32 pages depending on the story.

Can I create books for multiple children?

Absolutely! You can create separate books for each child, or for some of our stories, you can include multiple children in the same book. Just select the "multiple children" option during personalization.
