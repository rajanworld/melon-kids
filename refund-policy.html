Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

Satisfaction Guarantee

At MelonKids, we're committed to your complete satisfaction with our personalized books. We carefully craft each book with attention to detail to ensure the highest quality product. However, if you're not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Before Production: Orders can be cancelled at no charge before they enter the production phase, which typically happens within 2 hours of order confirmation.
  • During Production: Once your book has entered production, a 50% cancellation fee will apply to cover the costs already incurred.
  • After Shipping: Orders cannot be cancelled once they have been shipped. Please refer to our return policy instead.

To cancel an order, please contact our customer support team immediately through our contact page or email us at support@melonkids.com with your order number.

Return Policy

We accept returns under the following circumstances:

  • Printing Errors or Defects: If your book arrives with printing errors, binding issues, or other manufacturing defects, we will replace it at no cost to you or provide a full refund.
  • Damaged in Transit: If your book is damaged during shipping, we will replace it or provide a full refund.
  • Content Errors Due to Our Mistake: If there are errors in the content of your book due to our mistake (not related to the information you provided), we will replace it or provide a full refund.

Please note: Because personalized books are custom-made specifically for you, we cannot accept returns for content you approved during the design process or for any other reason not listed above.

Refund Process

To request a refund or replacement:

  1. Contact our customer support within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and describe the issue with your book.
  3. Include clear photos showing the defect or damage.

Once your return request is approved, we will either:

  • Send you a replacement book, or
  • Process a refund to your original payment method, which typically takes 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.

Shipping Costs for Returns

If the return is due to our error or a defect in the product, we will cover the cost of return shipping. In such cases, we will provide you with a prepaid shipping label.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Digital products or electronic gift cards
  • Products that have been personalized correctly according to your specifications
  • Shipping fees (except in cases where the product was defective or damaged during shipping)

Special Promotions and Discounts

Refunds for items purchased with a promotional discount will be adjusted to reflect the actual amount paid. If a refunded item was part of a bundle or special offer, the value of the remaining items may be recalculated at regular prices.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting to our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund & Cancellation Policy, please contact us at refunds@melonkids.com or through our contact page.
